Top 10 Movies

  1. Scary movie

    Introduction: Scary Movie is a fun comedy. In its first installment, the film parodies scenes from several horror films such as *I Know What You Did Last Summer* and *Scream*, but instead of the terrifying scenes of the original versions, the humor is present in every detail, every character, and every scene in the film, and will accompany the viewer from beginning to end. This is considered a worthy rival to *American Pie*.

  2. Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse

    Introduction: Miles Morales returns in the sequel to the Oscar-winning animated blockbuster, Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse. After reuniting with Gwen Stacy, the friendly Spider-Man from Brooklyn must travel through the multiverse and encounter a group of Spider-Men responsible for protecting parallel worlds. But when the superhero team clashes over how to handle a new threat, Miles is forced to go head-to-head with other Spider-Men and redefine what it means to be a hero in order to save those he loves most.

  3. Transformers One

    Introduction: This is the never-before-revealed origin story of Optimus Prime and Megatron. These two characters, known as sworn enemies but also once close brothers, changed the fate of Cybertron forever.

  4. Good Fortune

    Introduction: Gabriel, an angel with an abundance of kindness but a lack of skills, inadvertently interferes in the lives of a low-wage part-time worker and a wealthy venture capitalist, turning everything upside down.

  5. White Chicks

    Introduction: Following a failed mission, agents Kevin and Marcus Copeland have lost their reputation at work. To regain their honor, they decide to undertake a new mission: protecting the two Wilson sisters from a kidnapping plot.

  6. 404 Run Run

    Introduction: Nakrob, a young real estate con artist, discovers an abandoned hotel on a hillside near the beach. Seeing an opportunity, he decides to turn it into a luxury hotel scam. However, Nakrob soon realizes the hotel still has "residents"—the ghost of the original owner.

  7. Twilight of the Warriors: Walled In

    Introduction: Lac Quan illegally enters Hong Kong and is helped by Quyen Phong to survive in Kowloon Walled City – a complex area with many criminal gangs. During a time of trouble, they inadvertently discover the unspoken rules behind the chaos. Facing these criminal forces, they rise up against the dark schemes of their enemies to uphold their vow to protect the peace of the area.

  8. Exhuma

    Introduction: Two sorcerers, a feng shui master, and an embalmer join forces to excavate the cursed tomb of a wealthy family, aiming to save the last remaining descendant. The dark secrets of their ancestors are revealed.

  9. Death Whisperer

    Introduction: When a bloodthirsty spirit possesses a young girl in a remote village, her brother takes the lead in an effort to expel the spirit before she is overwhelmed by evil.

  10. Annabelle Comes Home

    Introduction: Hidden within the mysterious, time-worn room of paranormal explorers Ed and Lorraine Warren, the haunted doll Annabelle suddenly trembles violently, unleashing ferocious demonic spirits. Terrifying screams echo throughout the house, darkness descends, and Annabelle, with her ghostly eyes fixed on Ed and Lorraine's young daughter Judy, sows inexplicable terror and haunting.
